# Nikita Murasovs

Data scientist & robotics PhD. Machine learning, quantitative finance, crypto markets, and on-chain data.

I hold a PhD in Robotics from the University of Leeds and currently work as an Associate Data Scientist. Alongside my main role, I work as a WorldQuant Brain Research Consultant and conduct independent quantitative research.

## Current research

- Alpha and factor research
- Market microstructure and execution
- Crypto and on-chain analysis
- Statistical modelling and machine learning
- Blockchain infrastructure and smart contracts
- Risk management and strategy evaluation

## Technology stack

**Languages:**         Python, SQL, TypeScript  
**Data and ML:**       Pandas, NumPy, SciPy, scikit-learn, PyTorch, Matplotlib  
**Data engineering:**  PostgreSQL, APIs, WebSockets, ETL pipelines  
**Web:**               Next.js, React, Tailwind CSS  
**Research:**          Statistical modelling, time-series analysis, backtesting, optimisation
